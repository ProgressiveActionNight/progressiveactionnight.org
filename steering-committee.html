Steering Committee

The steering committee represents the people present in the hacknight. The steering committee discusses the general direction, culture, plans, and tone of the hacknight. We reachout to breakout group leaders and find ways to improve their work. The committee also makes sure that the hacknight is an inclusive and safe space to be in.

The steering committee meets every week. Contact us at steering [at] proghacknight [dot] org
