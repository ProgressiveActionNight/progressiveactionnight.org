Welcome!

Join us every other Tuesday from 6:30-9:30p on the 15th floor of ThoughtWorks NYC to hear from amazing speakers, discuss progressive issues and work on civic projects. Everyone is welcome!

Progressive HackNight is a bi-weekly gathering of coders and creatives, makers and doers, to foment and inspire action as well as use our collective talents and abilities to push forward progressive agendas and pro-social change in benefit of the common good. More about us »

Join our community

Prog Hacknight is a hub for New York's progressive tech community. Here's the three best ways to get involved:

1. Go to Prog Hacknight. We meet every other Tuesday at 6:30pm

2. Subscribe to updates on upcoming speakers and events

3. Browse our breakouts to find your group
